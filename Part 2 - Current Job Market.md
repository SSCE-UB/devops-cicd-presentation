
## DevOps Job Market in Iran and Technologies for DevOps Engineers

The DevOps job market in **Iran** is experiencing significant growth, with more companies adopting DevOps processes every day. Job listings for DevOps engineers are increasing, and the average salary for DevOps professionals ranges from **10 to 50 million Toman,** depending on the city and level of seniority.
